Findings
What today taught us
✗
Both losses were counter-trend BUYs into strong downtrends

The 14:00 BUY had H1 + M30 + M15 all trending down. The 16:20 BUY had M30 strongly trending down (ADX 41.8). The bot entered on M5 signals against the full higher TF picture.

!
M30 block doesn't help inside the current strategy

Standalone the M30 block looks good (+1.2% win rate). But combined with current filters it drops from 45.8% to 43.4%. The 50 setups it blocks have a 54% win rate — the strategy is actually picking good counter-trend entries. Adding the block removes the best ones.

!
Gold RSI zone is wrong for this asset

Gold had strong trending signals all day but the RSI zone filter (50–60 BUY / 40–50 SELL) blocked everything. Gold RSI goes more extreme than EURUSD — when trending, RSI is already past the window. This needs a gold-specific RSI threshold or removal of RSI for gold entirely.

✓
Session filter removal is already showing

Current strategy baseline is now 225 setups at 45.8% — up from 175 setups at 44.6% in v1. More entries, better win rate. The change made Monday 6 July.

Next
What to investigate for v2
Don't add M30 block. The data says no — it removes good trades. Leave the strategy as-is for now and collect more data.
Gold RSI: The RSI zone (50–60 / 40–50) is designed for EURUSD. For gold, either widen the window significantly (45–70 / 30–55) or run a separate test with RSI removed entirely for XAUUSD. Gold has been live but never traded — that's a wasted instrument.
